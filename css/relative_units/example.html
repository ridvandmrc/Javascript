Absolute Units

CM (centimeters) may be used for printers
MM (millimeter) may be used for printers or known exact width and height
px (pixels): one pixel equals 1/96th of in


Relative Units

Em: Relative to parent(section) font-size (parent-font-size is 32px) so 1em equals 32px
Rem: Relative to body font-size (body font-size is 16px) so 1rem equals 16px
